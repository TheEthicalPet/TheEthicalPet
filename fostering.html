Fostering a pet means to provide it with a loving and safe home until it can find a forever family. It gives animals a chance to temporarily escape their cage in shelters and provides them with human interaction. Maybe you will end up falling in love with the animal and end up adopting it.

Benefits of Fostering a Shelter Dog:

- You can foster a pet whenever it is convenient for you.
- You nurture the pet and provide a loving and safe home for animals possibly afraid of humans.
- Your provide it with an environment that has freedoms and is not as restricting as a cage.
- You have a hand in helping the dog become more adoptable because it will be more used to human interaction.
- You can help train the dog and watch it grow.
- You can change the dogs future.
- You build an unbreakable bond.
- You show foster pets that they are still lovable, no matter what they have been through.
- You are potentially saving a life.
- Fostering helps you get the closure you need after having lost a pet or someone close.
- You could potentially meet your new family member as you could fall in love and adopt the dog.
